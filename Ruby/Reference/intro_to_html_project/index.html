Protein Smoothie

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

The world famous Protein Smoothie Health Shake recipe that is tasty and rich in macro-micro nutrients. Ideal as a post/pre workout drink or as a meal replacement. Easy to prepare, quick to be devoured.

Ingredients

  • 350ml Milk
  • a cup of crushed ice
  • a cup of cut strawberries
  • 1 Scoop Strawberry Whey
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Directions

  1. Put the ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve with strawberries as garnishing
